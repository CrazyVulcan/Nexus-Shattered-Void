About the Game

NEXUS:
Shattered Void

Nexus: Shattered Void is an independent tabletop wargame created by a small team of passionate designers who love fleet combat, sci-fi worldbuilding, and crunchy tactical games.

The Game

Nexus: Shattered Void began as a home project born out of a desire for a fast-playing, deeply strategic fleet combat game that didn't require expensive miniatures or complex terrain to enjoy. The result is a system-agnostic ruleset that works with any ship models, 3D prints, or even simple tokens.

The game emphasizes asymmetric faction design—each of the four factions has a fundamentally different tactical identity that rewards players who master their chosen force. A Void Syndicate player wins through deception and speed; an Iron Dominion player wins through attrition and overwhelming firepower.

The Ship Builder tool and the open JSON data format were designed from the start to support community content—custom factions, ships, and campaigns are encouraged. All game data is openly formatted for modification.
