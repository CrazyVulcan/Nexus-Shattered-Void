What is Nexus: Shattered Void?

A modular tactical fleet combat game for 2–6 players. Build custom fleets, command diverse factions, and fight for dominance across the void.

🌌

The Setting

Ten thousand years ago, a cataclysmic weapon shattered the galactic core. The Nexus Fragments—drifting remnants of impossible energy—scattered across the void. Now four great powers fight over them, each believing the Fragments hold the key to absolute power.

The Gameplay

Alternate activation, dice-based combat with meaningful tactical decisions. Each ship has unique stats, weapon slots, and module bays. Fleet composition matters as much as tactics. Games take 60–120 minutes with fleets of 3–10 ships per side.
